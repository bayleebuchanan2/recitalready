Day of Show

Review the full schedule and important details so you know exactly where to be throughout recital day.

Run Down of the Day

Follow this schedule closely to ensure the day runs smoothly for everyone.

Dress Rehearsal

Dancers must arrive at Wilson Hall by 9:30 AM. Come ready with hair and makeup completed and all costumes packed. We will begin running dances right at 10:00 AM after a group stretch. This is when photographers will be taking photos, so it is important to smile and perform.

Lunch Break

After running through dances, dancers may go eat lunch on campus. However, you must return by 1:00 PM. Please do not leave campus.

Showtime

Doors open at 1:30 PM and the show begins at 2:00 PM. If you are in the first number, please be backstage by 1:50 PM.

Post-Show

After the show, feel free to mingle with family and friends. Please return all costumes to the collection bins and clean up your dressing room space.

Expectations

Please follow these expectations to ensure the recital runs smoothly and professionally.

General

  • If you do not show up to tech rehearsal without filling out the form or contacting an executive member, you will be fined $30 and will not be able to dance in recital.
  • If you have a gap of more than 2 or 3 dances, you are expected to sit in the reserved Dancing Dukes audience section once changed into your next costume.
  • Do not walk in or out of the auditorium during performances. Doors should only be opened between dances.

Backstage

  • You should be backstage and ready to go one full dance before your performance.
  • You should remain silent backstage. Noise carries into the auditorium.

Quick Changes

  • A quick change is when you have one or no dances between performances to change costumes.
  • If you have two or more dances between, you should change in your dressing room.
  • If you have a quick change, set your costume, shoes, and items backstage before the recital begins.
  • If you need help, assign someone before the show so there is no confusion.
