Hair, Makeup, and Accessories

There will be a recital emergency kit in the dressing rooms, but do not rely on it. Bring your own bobby pins, hairspray, hair ties, makeup, and anything else you need.

Hair

  • Slicked back low ponytail with a middle part
  • Use gel and hairspray so there are no flyaways
  • Hair is the same for all dances

Makeup

  • Focus on brows, lips, and eyes so stage lights do not wash you out
  • Natural smoky eye or subtle eyeshadow look
  • Red lips similar to the assigned shade
  • You do not have to use the exact lipstick, but try to match the shade closely
  • Mascara please, fake eyelashes are optional

Accessories

  • No necklaces
  • No hoops or dangling earrings
  • Simple earrings such as studs are acceptable
  • No bracelets or hair ties on your wrists
  • Nail color does not matter
